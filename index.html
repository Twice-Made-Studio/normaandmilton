Our Story

How two hearts found each other

Her Story

It was November 15, 2024 – the height of holiday shopping madness at Target. I was working the register, exhausted from the endless stream of customers. Then Milton walked into my line. The first time, I thought nothing of it. The second time, I noticed his smile. By the third time that week, I realized he was specifically choosing my line just to make me laugh with his terrible dad jokes. What started as eye rolls at his corny humor slowly turned into genuine smiles, then exchanged numbers, then coffee dates, and before I knew it, I had fallen completely in love with the man who made Target's holiday rush the best season of my life.

His Story

He swears he noticed her first… and maybe he did. Milton claims it was love at first sight the moment he walked into Target that November day. "I only needed batteries," he says, "but I kept finding reasons to come back." Three shopping trips in one week? Suspicious. A cart full of random items just to have an excuse to stand in her line? Absolutely. He admits the jokes were a desperate attempt to see her smile. "I knew I had to make her laugh – it was the only way she'd remember me." Well, Milton, mission accomplished. She remembers everything.

He Said / She Said

Who fell first?
She says: "He was a goner by visit number two."
He says: "Guilty as charged. Visit one, actually."
Who was googly-eyed?
She says: "Him. Definitely him. I was professional."
He says: "We were both googly-eyed. She just hid it better."
Who made the first move?
She says: "I wrote my number on his receipt. Bold, I know."
He says: "She did — and I still have that receipt."
