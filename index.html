Our Story

Two people, one Target line, and a holiday rush that turned into a lifetime of inside jokes.

Norma’s Story

It was November 15, 2024 — the middle of the holiday rush at Target. I was working the register, trying to keep up with the endless line of shoppers, when I noticed him. Milton. He kept coming back… always in my line, even when others were shorter.

At first, I thought he was just indecisive about his shopping. But then I realized he was trying to make me laugh — and somewhere between gift wrap and peppermint mochas, something shifted.
