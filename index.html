Stories

Our Story

Two people, one Target line, and a holiday rush that turned into a lifetime of inside jokes.

Norma’s Story

It was November 15, 2024, and Target was in full holiday chaos. I was at my register, doing my best to keep the line moving and the spirits bright, when I noticed the same face popping up again and again. Milton kept choosing my line—no matter how long it was.

At first, I thought it was a coincidence. Then I realized he was absolutely doing it on purpose, armed with a new joke or comment every time. Somewhere between the gift wrap, the peppermint mochas, and his terrible-but-charming one-liners, something shifted.

What started as quick moments at checkout turned into real conversations, then into dates, and eventually into a love that felt both brand new and like it had always been there. Looking back, I’m so glad he kept getting “just one more thing.”

Milton’s Story

Milton insists he noticed her first—and that he was definitely not “shopping” at Target that often on purpose. His full version is coming soon, but for now, he would like it on record that he had a plan, even if it looked a lot like wandering the seasonal aisle for the fifth time that week.

What he will admit is this: every trip felt worth it the moment he saw her smile.
